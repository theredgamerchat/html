Giggle-inducing Pasta

"Prepare to giggle your way to pasta heaven with this whimsical dish! Our Giggle-inducing Pasta is a delightful blend of absurdity and flavor."

Ingredients:

  • Unicorn tears (1 cup)
  • Dragon scales (2 tablespoons)
  • Fairy giggles (3 teaspoons)
  • Stardust (a pinch)
  • Mermaid whispers (1 tablespoon)

Cooking Instructions:

  • Boil a cauldron of water and add the unicorn tears.
  • Stir in the dragon scales until they shimmer like a disco ball.
  • Sprinkle the fairy giggles and stardust, and watch your pasta twirl with joy.
  • Simmer the mermaid whispers until they harmonize into a melodious sauce.
  • Serve with a side of laughter and enjoy!
