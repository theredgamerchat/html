Space Exploration Glossary

Aberration of light
Phenomenon where stars appear slightly off from their true position due to Earth's motion interacting with the speed of light.
Alpha Centauri
Closest star system to Earth, comprising Alpha Centauri A, Alpha Centauri B, and Proxima Centauri.
Andromeda Galaxy
Closest galaxy to the Milky Way, visible with the unaided eye on fall and winter nights.
Asteroid
Chunks of rock or metal orbiting the sun, remnants of small planets that broke apart due to collisions.
