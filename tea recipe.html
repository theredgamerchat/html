How to make tea

ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Milk
    1. soy milk
    2. cow milk
    3. almond milk
    4. holy milk
    5. suspisous milk
  • water
  • sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil some water
  2. prepare hot milk
  3. add some milk
  4. add some tea leaves
  5. leave on stove till creamy brown color acheived
  6. add sugar if you want

Hope you will enjoy your tea
